Hangman the Game

The hangman game is a classic, albeit morbid, pen and paper game where you attempt to guess a random word before running out of guesses. Each incorrect guess results in another body part added to the hanging person image. If you guess the word before the image is completed you win. But if the image is completed before you guess the word, you lose.

In this version of hangman you must complete the word before you complete six parts of the hanging person. You can choose letters and they will fill in for the word. When the word is completed you will be declared a winner. You can also reset the game at any time with the New Word button. Click on the New Word button to begin the game.

Have fun!
